Who wrote it

Shailesh Lodha aka Tarak Mehta is one of the most respected person in the Gokuldham Society. His wife, Anjali Mehta is also known as caring wife. But people wants to know about Tarak Mehta aka Shailesh Lodha’s real life. That’s why we have written this article to provide information on Shailesh Lodha and his real life.

Shailesh Lodha was born in 15th July, 1969 in Jodhpur city of Rajasthan. Besides acting in the TV serials, he is comedian as well as writer in real life. Besides Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah, he is also appearing in “Waah Waah Kya Baat Hai” with his reel life wife Anjali Mehta.

Besides acting and comedy, he has written four books as writer. First two books written on satirical humour, third one self-help book and fourth one is collection of poems. This fourth book written with his wife as co-author.

Shailesh Lodha is also much popular as comedian and best film actor in Rajasthan. Recently, he went to Banswara in Rajasthan for stage performance. A huge crowd of almost 40,000 people waiting for him. Shailesh Lodha didn’t able to reach stage immediately due to huge crowd. It takes almost half an hour to reach at stage to start his performance. Due to this love and appreciation, Shailesh Lodha always happy to see them.

Due to his popularity, Shailesh Lodha has also explained that he didn’t celebrated Holi with his family since 20 years. Most of time, he always busy with shows across the World. This proves how much Shailesh Lodha is popular among people across India as well as around the World. He got skyrocket popularity due to Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah TV soap.
